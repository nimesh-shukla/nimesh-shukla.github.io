Intro

What I am all about.

Highly motivated, self-driven and results-oriented researcher in Fluorescence spectroscopy/Ultrafast time resolved measurements /Nonlinear optics/Lasers. Team player with effective communication, strong problem-solving skills, critical thinking, high technical creativity and scientific curiosity, ability to meet deadlines and targets, and excellent academic background with PhD in Physics (Wesleyan University) and B.Tech. in Electrical engineering (IIT Dhanbad, India).

Professional Skills and Qualifications
: Successfully implemented various interdisciplinary projects ranging from optical spectroscopy, computational modeling to characterization of bio-molecules throughout my research career.

: Technical hands on experience of multiple optics and spectroscopic techniques i.e. pump-probe nonlinear spectroscopy,ultrafast femtosecond fluorescence up-conversion, time resolved measurements, time correlated single photon counting, circular dichroism and UV-Vis absorption/emission spectroscopy,high-energy pulsed lasers and automation.

: Familiarity with multiple advance optics and spectroscopic techniques i.e. Nonlinear parametric amplification, 2D spectroscopy, ultrafast pulse shaping, FTIR and THz spectroscopy.

: Experience of various electronics/optoelectronics i.e. lock-in Amplifier, gated integrator, photon counters, ultrafast pulse pickers, PMT, photodiodes, DC and AC circuits using linear transistors, integrated circuits and digital integrated circuits.

: Solid knowledge of nonlinear and geometrical optics, experimental and theoretical physics, atomic and molecular optics and quantum mechanics.

:High performance computing cluster experience with proficiency in LabVIEW, MATLAB/Simulink, C, Fortran, Autodock VINA, LAMMPS, Gaussian, ChemDraw, Origin, MS office, CorelDraw, GIMP.

: Interested strongly in R&D and prototyping, disruptive innovations and technology integration, entrepreneurial thinking, collaborative working in Interdisciplinary teams.
